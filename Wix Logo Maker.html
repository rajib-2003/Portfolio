Frequently Asked Questions

Which files do I need if I want to print my logo?
Vector (SVG) files are scalable and best for printing. You can print your logo on something small like a business card, or on something huge like a billboard — while keeping the same high quality. You get SVG files when you choose the Advanced Logo package, Unlimited or Business Logo+Website plans.
Can I make changes to my logo after I download it?
With a Value or Just Logo plan (both Basic and Advanced), you can make any changes you want only until your first download. After downloading, you cannot make any changes to your logo. With an Unlimited or Business plan, you can make any design changes you want at any time (before AND after download), but the business name in your logo will be locked after your first download.
Can I download a logo for free?
Yes, but you’ll only be able to download a low-resolution sample file. To get high quality logo files and full commercial usage rights, you’ll need to purchase your logo.
Can I get a website with my logo?
Yes. When you purchase your logo, you get a website designed just for you, using the style and color palette of your new logo. With a Value, Unlimited or Business Logo Plan, your site will be upgraded with a Premium Plan, so you get a free custom domain for 1 year, extra storage, great SEO and more.
How do I renew my logo plan?
Once you purchase any plan, the logo files are yours to keep. Logo + Website plans are automatically renewed each year to ensure uninterrupted service.
How do I pay for my logo?
You can pay using any major credit or debit card, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, JCB and Diners. Additional payment options may be available in your specific location.
What types of files do I get?
Some plans include standard .png files, while others include these plus resizable, social media, business card, and social posts files. See the full breakdown of file types in each plan.
